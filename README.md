# ClearPath.ai — First-Gen Career Intelligence Atlas 🏆

> **Submission for Hack2skill Virtual Prompt War 2026**
> Developed at **BhuuX Studio** | Team: Bhushan & Kumar Swamy

ClearPath.ai is a smart, dynamic career assistant designed specifically for **First-Generation Indian students.** It provides personalized roadmaps, state-specific aid data, and professional networking tools to bridge the information gap in semi-urban and rural India.

## 🎯 1. Chosen Vertical
**Vertical:** First-Generation Student Career Mentor / Financial Aid Strategy.
**Mission:** Empowering students whose families lack institutional knowledge of modern high-growth careers.

## 🧠 2. Approach & Logic
Our solution uses a **Multi-Track Decision Matrix** based on:
*   **State-Level Intelligence:** A custom atlas of scholarships for all 28 Indian states.
*   **Education Silos:** Dynamic branching for Science (Tech/AI) vs. Non-Science (FinTech/Commerce).
*   **Socio-Economic Anchors:** A "Parental Mode" that translates tech-jargon into trust-based metrics (Security & Salary) to help families make joint decisions.
*   **Regional Economics:** Real-time COL (Cost of Living) estimates for 8 major Indian tech hubs.

## ⚙️ 3. How the Solution Works
1.  **User Profiling:** The wizard captures the user's name, home state, and education track.
2.  **AI Intelligence Scan:** The system queries the **BhuuX Ultra-Massive Data Atlas** (500+ hard-coded strategic data points) to find the highest-ROI path.
3.  **Dynamic Rendering:** The PWA engine generates a 3-step execution sprint, a skill-gap analysis, and pre-written networking scripts.
4.  **Offline-First:** Using a Service Worker (`sw.js`), the app works anywhere, even without internet.

## 🌐 4. Google Services Integration
To ensure a premium and connected experience, we integrated:
*   **Google Calendar API (Sync):** Every career milestone in the roadmap has a "Sync to Google Calendar" button, allowing students to schedule their sprints directly into their personal Google account.
*   **Google Fonts API:** Optimized typography using the 'Plus Jakarta Sans' and 'Inter' families for maximum readability and a premium SaaS aesthetic.
*   **Chrome V8 Performance:** Optimized JavaScript execution for high-speed rendering on Google Chrome.

## 🛡️ 5. Assumptions & Considerations
*   **Data Sourcing:** Scholarship data is based on 2025-2026 state-level government portals (Bihar Student Credit Card, MAHA-DBT, etc.).
*   **Salary Benchmarks:** ROI projections assume entry-level roles in Tier-1 and Tier-2 Indian tech ecosystems.
*   **Accessibility:** We assume the user may face bandwidth constraints; hence the Offline-First PWA architecture.
